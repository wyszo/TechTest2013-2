People

A unique blend of specialist app strategists, designers and engineers.

The company was founded by a team who spent three years working for Apple, launching and marketing the iPhone, iPad, App Store and hundreds of apps.

At the App Business we combine a unique understanding of audiences, brands and technology to help clients figure out what they should do, not just what they could do, with this new technology. Our strategy, design and engineering specialists work side by side in flexible teams, enabling us to quickly respond to client briefs and adapt to emerging technologies.
